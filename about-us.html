Frequently Asked Questions

What makes MOVA different from other ecommerce stores? ▼

MOVA stands out through our rigorous product curation, premium customer service, fast worldwide shipping, and unwavering commitment to quality. Every product is handpicked and tested to ensure it meets our exacting standards before reaching our customers.

How long does shipping take? ▼

Most orders ship within 2-3 business days. Delivery times vary by location but typically range from 5-15 business days internationally. You'll receive real-time tracking updates for your order.

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, simply initiate a return through your account and we'll provide a prepaid shipping label. Full refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.

Is my payment information secure? ▼

Absolutely. We use SSL encryption and industry-leading security protocols to protect your personal and financial information. MOVA is PCI-DSS compliant and never stores your complete credit card details.

How can I track my order? ▼

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to monitor your package's journey in real-time through our website or the carrier's tracking page.
